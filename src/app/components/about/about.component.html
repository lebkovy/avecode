About Me
Hello, I'm Jakub

I graduated from the Polish-Japanese Academy of Information Technology in Gdansk, specialising in web applications within the Faculty of Computer Science. My fascination with the intricate workings of the IT world has been a driving force throughout my academic journey. Starting from a background in computer graphics and dabbling briefly in video editing, I eventually committed to pursuing a more substantial path in IT, driven by the prospect of exciting career prospects in the future.

Aside from my academic pursuits, my other great passion is traveling. It's a fundamental part of my life, and I can't recall a holiday without venturing abroad. I've explored extensively throughout the Balkans, Spain, France, Italy, the Benelux countries, North Africa, Israel, Turkey, the Baltic region, and even China.

I strive to demonstrate that a love for travel and IT can harmoniously coexist. It's a unique experience to code while surrounded by the Great Wall of China, the tranquil waters of the Dead Sea, or the breathtaking scenery of the Cote d'Azur. I highly recommend this blend of exploration and technological immersion to everyone!
